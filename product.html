Frequently Asked Questions

Q:What are kangkong chips?
A: Kangkong chips are a crispy snack made from deep-fried water spinach (kangkong) leaves, typically coated in a batter of egg and flour, originating from the Philippines.

Q:What does kangkong chips taste like
A: The taste of kangkong chips is often described as slightly earthy with a hint of sweetness, similar to the taste of fresh kangkong, with the added crispiness from frying.
